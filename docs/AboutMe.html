About Me

Who I Am

I'm passionate about game design and development — blending creativity, technical skill, and storytelling to build engaging, meaningful experiences. I’m especially interested in working with studios or design consultancies like SPARCK, where innovation and player-centered design matter most.

During my Computer Science Games Technology course at Nottingham Trent University, I’ve built a strong foundation in programming and interaction design. One of my favourite projects involved developing a C++ game using SFML, where I implemented procedural maze generation, interactive UI, and a simple scoring system — alongside designing my own textures to give the project a personal touch.

Guest lectures and coursework have helped me appreciate the importance of collaboration, continuous learning, and clear communication in the industry. I’m actively building on these skills through personal projects and upcoming placements, with the goal of becoming a developer who makes games that others can lose themselves in — just like I have.
